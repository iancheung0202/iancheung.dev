About Me

I am a website developer who was born in Hong Kong and now living in California. I am currently 15 years old and studying in high school. Besides website developing, my favourite hobbies include playing the violin, the piano and some sports like cycling. I have advanced music technique. Given that, I have won lots of local advanced competitions and recently passed and was awarded Merit in the LTCL Performance diploma exam in Trinity College, London. Besides that, I am also an exceptionally knowledgable video editor.

I personally believe that I am no ordinary student. Noticing that I was having trouble continuing my same life which has been on-going for a few years already, therefore, I suspected I should start to learn a new technique. As a result, in the evenings and weekends I started to build websites. Having done coding since I was small, I had actually never considered it to be something I would greatly pursue, but instead something on the side for fun. As time goes on, the aspects of being a developer became more intriguing, most notably being able to create a product I or someone could use, and as well allow me to apply my entrepreneurship skills led me to make the switch over to pursue to be a professional website developer.

Passionate about learning more about development outside of class to accelerate my learning and improve my skills, this website is used for me to learn more about front-end web development; and as well showcase the projects that I complete.

Some things I'm proud of in my journey in switching so far:

  • Creating my own personal mini-game (Snake Game), enhancing my JavaScript knowledge, as well as the front-end langauges including responsive CSS
  • Developing my own website
  • Developing a multi-purpose ultility Discord Bot with various functions such as ticket tool
