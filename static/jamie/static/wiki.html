Jamie Cheung is a 16-year-old high school student, classical violist, and aspiring polymath. He is known for his interest in geopolitics, biochemistry, and linguistics.

"You won't improve unless you struggle in practice."
— Jamie, 2025

Biography

Jamie lives in the Holocene Epoch. He is an avid reader and believes in the importance of lifelong learning. A native Cantonese speaker, he is also studying French. He identifies as a HongKonger.

Interests

  • Academics: Geopolitics, geography, biology, chemistry, and history.
  • Music: Classical music performance on viola, violin, and piano.
  • Advocacy: Support for green energy.
  • Extracurriculars: Science Olympiad, debate, and Model United Nations.
